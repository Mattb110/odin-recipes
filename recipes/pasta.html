Pasta

Description

This easy spaghetti and meat sauce recipe takes just a few minutes of prep and then a low simmer on the stove for a deep, meaty-flavored, homemade spaghetti for the best family favorite dinner.

ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Beef broth
  • Canned mushrooms
  • A splash of wine
  • Onion, celery and fresh garlic
  • Dried herbs
  • kosher salt
  • ground black pepper
  • pinch of sugar
  • Dried whole cloves

Steps

  1. Brown the beef. Drain off any excess fat, then, throw in the chopped celery, onion, and garlic and cook until the vegetables soften.

  2. Boil and simmer. Dump the rest of the homemade pasta sauce ingredients into the pot and bring the mixture to a boil. Once the spaghetti sauce is boiling, reduce the heat and let it simmer on the stove for 3 hours. I know 3 hours sounds like forever, but thats how long it takes to make a rich, flavorful meat sauce.
